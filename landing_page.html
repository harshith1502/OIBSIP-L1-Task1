LET'S EXPLORE GOA

ACTIVITIES

  • Blive

India's first Electric bike tours. Experience the joy of effortless riding on E-bikes while exploring the hidden gems of Goa.

Get ready to be blown away with the rich marine diversity and over 100 species of fish that Goa has to offer as we take you on a magical boat ride with your friends and family on an 8 hour excursion on the sea. Sight delightful dolphins on the way to Grandé Island where you get to scuba dive and snorkel in amazing dive sites like Suzy’s Wreck, Coral Garden, Bounty Bay, Lobsters’ Avenue, and Shelter Cove.

  • Scuba Diving
  • Hot Air Balloon

Hot air ballooning is one of the safest, and simplest, forms of flight known to man! At Tiger Balloon Safaris your safety is our number one priority and our paramount responsibility.

Enjoy the thrilling bungee jumping adventure at Mayem Lake, which is a charming tiny village located in North Goa and is enclosed by a thick forest.
